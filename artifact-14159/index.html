Purpose

This archive is not instructional. It is a record of attempts — successful and failed — to understand systems designed to conceal meaning. Some entries are formal. Others are speculative. Many contradict earlier conclusions.

I no longer remember when this stopped being curiosity and became necessity. What matters is that the same ideas recur across cultures that never communicated, using tools separated by thousands of years.
